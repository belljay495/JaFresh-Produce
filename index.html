Our Story

About JaFresh Produce

JaFresh Produce was founded by a group of UTech students who noticed a broken system: Jamaican supermarkets import produce from abroad while small local farmers struggled to sell their crops.

We built this platform to bridge that gap — connecting farm families directly with households and restaurants across Jamaica. Every purchase puts more money in a Jamaican farmer's pocket and fresher food on your table.

Registered in Kingston, Jamaica. Proudly Jamaican 🇯🇲
