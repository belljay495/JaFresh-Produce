Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

JaFresh Produce is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email, phone, and delivery address when you register or order

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To manage your account and subscriptions

  • To improve our platform

3. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption and industry-standard security practices to protect your data.
